## Critical Anti-Patterns (do NOT repeat these)
- Boolean intersect check for movable solids → sticking. Use overlap-area delta.
- Keybinds for tool switching → contradicts Stardew-style inventory-driven tools. Active hotbar slot is the source of truth.
- Hardcoded attack facing → broken in 3 of 4 directions. Always compute facing from dominant axis of `playerPos - selfPos`.
- Single-texture atlas assumption → tools on separate sheet need multi-texture lookup. Use `_atlas.GetTexture(spriteId)`.
